FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before reaching out to us — built from real client conversations.

Booking & reservations

How do I make a reservation?+
Contact us via Instagram or WhatsApp with 3–4 preferred dates, the number of people, and the number of concepts you want. We will reply with available time slots. Your booking is only confirmed once the reservation fee is received — please send payment promptly as slots fill up in real time.
Do I need to decide the number of concepts before booking?+
Yes — we allocate studio time based on concept count, so this must be confirmed when booking. You can add more concepts later if you contact us before the shoot and we adjust your time slot. Most clients who get professional hair & makeup take 2–4 concepts to make the most of the experience.
What is a "concept"?+
One concept = one outfit + one lighting and backdrop setting. Each concept takes 20–30 minutes, during which 100–200 photos are captured. After the shoot, you select which photos you want retouched.
Can I book the photoshoot first and add hair & makeup later?+
Yes. If you are undecided about H&M, you can book the photoshoot first. However, we recommend confirming H&M at the same time, as our affiliated shops also fill up quickly and we need to book them on your behalf.

Payment & fees

How do I pay the reservation deposit?+
We accept: PayPal (use "Friends & Family" to minimize fees), WISE (recommended), KEB Hana bank transfer (+10,000 won for overseas transfers), and AliPay (+11.5% surcharge). Direct credit card payments are not accepted for deposits, but you can link a card to PayPal or WISE.

Please notify us immediately after payment and include the depositor's name.
How much is the reservation deposit?+

The exact amount varies by H&M shop. We will confirm the total when finalizing your booking. The deposit is fully deducted from your balance.
How do I pay the remaining balance on the day?+
The balance is split: the H&M balance is paid directly to the hair and makeup shop, and the photoshoot balance is paid at OCTOBEE STUDIO. We recommend cash or WISE — credit card payments at the studio add 10% VAT. We tell you the exact balances after receiving your deposit.
Can multiple people in a group pay separately?+
Yes. Group members can each pay their own portion via PayPal or WISE. Just make sure each person sends their depositor name to us right away after paying.

Hair & makeup

Is hair & makeup included in the photoshoot price?+
No — it is a separate service booked at an affiliated shop. We handle the reservation on your behalf at a discounted rate. If you are unsure which shop suits your style and budget, we are happy to recommend one.
Can I skip hair & makeup and do my own?+
Yes, it is optional. That said, we strongly recommend professional H&M — the quality of hair and makeup has a significant impact on the final photos. If you decide to do your own, just let us know when booking so we do not reserve an H&M slot for you.
Can I get makeup only, without hair styling?+
Yes, makeup-only is available at most shops. Please specify this when booking — shops prepare in advance and once booked as full hair & makeup, it cannot be changed to makeup-only (or hair-only) on the day of the appointment.
Can members of a group each choose a different H&M shop?+
Yes. Each person can choose a different affiliated shop. We coordinate all the reservations so everyone arrives at the studio at the same time.
Are there early start fees?+
Yes — shops charge a surcharge for starts before their normal opening time. SHEJAC opens at 8:00 AM (no early fee). SHINOKI SAENAL and S.TOB open at 10:00 AM. Yoning at 9:00 AM, A-BY-BOM at 10:00 AM. We display the full early start fee schedule in the Hair & Makeup section above, and will tell you the exact surcharge when confirming your booking.
Do the H&M artists speak English?+
Yes — all our affiliated shops regularly serve international clients and can communicate in English. Bringing a reference photo to show the artist on the day is always recommended.
How should I prepare before going to the H&M shop?+
  • Arrive with clean skin and basic skincare only — no makeup. Sunscreen is fine.
  • Shampoo your hair at home beforehand — shampoo at the shop is an extra charge and takes more time.
  • Do not style your hair before arriving.
  • Bring reference photos of your desired look to show the artist.
  • Arrive on time — late arrivals may be treated as a no-show.
  • When arriving at the shop, mention you are going to OCTOBEE STUDIO to receive the affiliated discount.

At the studio

Where is OCTOBEE STUDIO?+
518-16 B1, Sinsa-dong, Gangnam-gu, Seoul. A 3-minute walk from Exit 8 of Sinsa Station (Line 3 / Sinbundang Line), next to Garosu-gil.

Traffic in Gangnam can be heavy. Please depart at least 30 minutes earlier than expected.
What happens on the day of the shoot?+
Arrive → Outfit change → Photoshoot → Photo selection → Payment → Check-out.

Each concept takes 20–30 minutes with 100–200 photos captured. After shooting, we give you time to select which photos to retouch. Please arrive 5–10 minutes early — lateness reduces your shooting and selection time.
Are outfits provided?+
Yes — we have a large selection of rental outfits in various sizes, including casual, professional, and editorial styles (shirts, blazers, and more), all free to rent. You are also welcome to bring your own clothes. Our photographer can recommend outfits that suit your look.
Do I need to choose a concept in advance?+
No — you can choose on the day and our photographer will recommend options based on your style. Browsing our Instagram (@octobee_film) in advance and saving reference photos is encouraged but not required.
Can I bring a companion?+
Yes. Please let us know in advance if anyone accompanying you will not be photographed, as space is limited. They are welcome to watch and take behind-the-scenes photos.
Do you offer professional or LinkedIn-style photos?+
Yes — business upper-body portraits are available. The studio has shirts and blazers to rent. Let us know your purpose when booking and our photographer will set up accordingly.

Photos & delivery

When will I receive my photos?+
Retouched photos are sent to your email within approximately 15 days, processed in order of shoot dates. For faster delivery, the express retouching add-on (50,000 won per concept) guarantees delivery within 3 days.

Photos are sent in a password-protected ZIP file — we send the password separately. Please download within 30 days as the link expires after that.
How many edited photos do I receive?+
1 concept = 3 photos · 2 concepts = 7 photos · 3 concepts = 11 photos · 4 concepts = 15 photos · 5 concepts = 19 photos.

You choose which photos to retouch after the shoot. Additional retouching beyond your package is 50,000 won per extra photo.
Can I get the original, unedited files?+
Yes — full HD original files are available as an add-on for 50,000 won per concept. You can decide after the shoot whether you want them; no commitment required in advance.
